---
title: "Adding a default sender to your domains"
description: "Add a default sender to your domains to improve email deliverability"
---

_To start sending emails from your account, you first need to add your domain and set a default sender. This ensures your messages will be delivered correctly and appear professional to recipients. Continue reading to learn how to do this in seconds._

Navigate to the **Settings** menu and open the **Domains** page. You should first [add your domain](https://help.hiredata.com/en/articles/285387-how-to-verify-a-domain-in-hiredata-to-send-emails) and then open it to see the field to add the default sender of your emails.

![Domains page](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/lk3ftrxl/48006898/8c54c944312aaf0e78ebc014d7cf/adding+email.png?expires=1776880800&signature=ea5a9f7759f251570f239fc227250c6545bc84bd956542cfd594f70c2571fbdf&req=1N1vxV7ypT9k2hj99dpg6tZ71nKaUedBoLqr3qLin9VUgHiBzsGUPuvz2Ip7%0A6qOKs2uq7TBq02bID3dbchOq%0A)

There are two ways to add a default sender:

**1. Selecting a synced user from the drop-down menu**

![Default sender user drop-down menu](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/lk3ftrxl/48007073/c9165cb0e1654ec9172a05243971/adding+email.png?expires=1776880800&signature=371c81aa418d034b00967520b4dc52e21ec13317decd6a266ff3f65e2ddefd15&req=1N1vxV%2F6qzRk2hj99dpg6sDoR2BQWb39hCS3TGc9WiCVtj0g4cc0nMrxWnj5%0AVWom2t3KXdDNr2Sr%2BFGBAGlp%0A)

**2. Manually typing their name and email address**

![Default sender's name and email address fields](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/lk3ftrxl/48007227/b60f55245686620515a6f3848f23/Screenshot+2025-03-25+at+16_37_11.png?expires=1776880800&signature=59f6207cdd423ad215e16a4ba7c384b2bca711eae81cd3cb5b84ce1f1286eb61&req=1N1vxV%2F4rjBk2hj99dpg6mrcOkQTS9oxunNdid8zjGRdzZAbO8G7OkQ7IoVb%0A3YVpQ1yqm2c7A5bnf5bK9slq%0A)

Note that the user who will be used to send emails from this domain should have the same email domain. If you have multiple domains in your account, each of them can have a different default sender; simply follow the steps explained in this guide.
