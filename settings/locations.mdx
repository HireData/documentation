_Welcome to our concise guide on setting up a location for your business in HireData. We have created an integration with Google Maps that allows you to add one or multiple locations for your business, but to use it, your company must be listed on [Google Business](https://www.google.com/intl/en_uk/business/), so if you haven't registered your profile yet, please do this before you continue with this guide._

## Step 1: Navigate to the Locations menu

Log in to your HireData profile, go to the settings menu located above your name and open the [Locations] tab.

![Locations menu](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/14627157/12def0e1fc8fef52dca83c58/image.png?expires=1776771000&signature=913ce407853f618f3db5a27b3a356543aede97712ca24375f17e9275f07b2520&req=0dFpx1%2F7qTBk2hL085ZhoZLyy0B6iCW5FqskRGNH8ITFWVoDvAZFLaxCrMS%2B%0A7Q%3D%3D%0A)

## Step 2: Add a new location

_2.1. Click on the [New Location] button:_

![New Location button](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/14627165/5a161abec25d1f48cfa88a25/image.png?expires=1776771000&signature=5445aceb2bb7a255901cfa7939a50de8544c7782b0f9fe4ba337d4df7b89e3ce&req=0dFpx1%2F7qjJk2hL085ZhoR1QUmQyLS8dwDQvwffQEEdTCv4PX3mza3X9k7E9%0A5w%3D%3D%0A)

_2.2. Name the location:_

![Name Location field](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/14627188/4787aeab12a03365cc8c6731/image.png?expires=1776771000&signature=515d64ce8362039d8fb8b2d7885484fc5bf5fbfe717a2b38d41b376aabbb85bc&req=0dFpx1%2F7pD9k2hL085ZhoapqK5Gv5FqD9n1m3RaK7WPSepBlALjnZ90bvkxr%0A7g%3D%3D%0A)

_2.3. Select its location from the results:_

![Location results](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/14627214/607094345da30ef6d95ab95f/image.png?expires=1776771000&signature=d48f0a82299988fc77112ab9692eae8cdb389fd86cd088f7c721b0f988649226&req=0dFpx1%2F4rTNk2hL085ZhoQLfcCDngNfaDbj7p9r1KeckpW5GUjjNR03WquiT%0AoQ%3D%3D%0A)

_2.4. Click on the [Create location] button_

![Create Location button](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/14627221/b82be4ae6fc1b83d4c7e0691/image.png?expires=1776771000&signature=8d480efb1c43da5970f457be7903700591cd231620de72489c1f82c0ce68da0b&req=0dFpx1%2F4rjZk2hL085ZhobWRERkixmGb8Zqp%2FI0X0hpAdXHn%2BArF2AS%2BA1%2B8%0ARA%3D%3D%0A)

_2.5. Review the location_

![Location overview](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/14627248/f124ba4300ee0b3ecd56ec02/image.png?expires=1776771000&signature=18b712cf8a42c72e7233257c6a33082819d3367fe65b9613e1ef57b1cfce3d22&req=0dFpx1%2F4qD9k2hL085ZhoSC5iTpvmM3GTPk9778ge76QGjFeEIQZgyhh2eKh%0A6A%3D%3D%0A)

## Pro Tip

Once your location is added to your HireData account, you will automatically get a Share Google Review URL, which you can send to others to collect native Google reviews. These types of reviews always perform better as a result of Google's algorithm.

![Drop-down menu with Share Google Review URL button](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/14627254/ffaa39d983be6017038e73d3/image.png?expires=1776771000&signature=c8599ad01fb9abd483bf3b1cf85a604f2e32587e1fa38a75edbdc3f4aec63ee8&req=0dFpx1%2F4qTNk2hL085ZhoS0%2F8m9onCRTFN%2FQ95DArQt%2FTLgTa0RS22M0%2BasV%0AWg%3D%3D%0A)

Finally, you can also [connect your brand to the location](https://help.hiredata.com/en/articles/157203-brands). That way, the location of your business will be automatically displayed in the communication you use, e.g. emails, where necessary.
