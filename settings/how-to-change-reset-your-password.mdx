---
title: "How to change/reset your password"
description: "Learn how to change or reset your HireData password for better security"
---

_If you've forgotten your password or simply want to update it for security reasons, you're in the right place. This guide will walk you through the steps to reset or change your password quickly and securely. Whether you're locked out or just taking proactive steps to protect your account, the process is simple and only takes a few minutes._

<Accordion title="Changing your Password while Logged in">

Navigate to the **Settings** menu and open your **Profile** page. Then, enter your new password, confirm it, and click [Change Password].

![Changing your password](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/lk3ftrxl/48005850/3a7119aec5b862ac168a5e05fbaa/Screenshot+2025-05-04+at+16_39_02.png?expires=1776771000&signature=503b8d29d84ed0c3c37e2e967d90370229cdcbef50abdfca263762b6ff33b6d3&req=1N1vxV3yqTdk2hL085ZhoWxjsAO%2FL2TCP04CGpP9xaDP6HDx4CnYakclZBUu%0A3Q%3D%3D%0A)

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Resetting your Password">

Head to the [HireData log-in page](https://app.hiredata.com/login) and click the [Forgot your Password?] redirect link.

![Forgot your password redirect link](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/lk3ftrxl/48006054/dd3acee7aecb0ba528fe46ebe3d2/Screenshot+2025-05-04+at+16_40_34.png?expires=1776771000&signature=2e219ab86f49a1ebbee0bd034ecdc1fcb3159fda67380f23fd9fcc01155db585&req=1N1vxV76qTNk2hL085ZhoS1zYcyY%2BZCrdGMmucRLLSurlLed2PtULBow36U4%0ABg%3D%3D%0A)

Enter the email address you used to register in HireData and click on [Reset Password].

![Reset password button](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/lk3ftrxl/48006116/2e219b70be8d85d4a6233dc9c3f9/Screenshot+2025-05-04+at+16_40_49.png?expires=1776771000&signature=d4ed31d610bf2a99baa105f4c3c9e4365d54cbf7a759bfed1f7930793acf9d2f&req=1N1vxV77rTFk2hL085ZhoRN%2F2LUyF2KJrCviYC%2FRkjQRIj8%2BYBKLEzV4ccSV%0Auw%3D%3D%0A)

Then open your email inbox, where you should receive an email from us with a link to change your Password.

![Email with reset password link](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/lk3ftrxl/48006141/6e7215a0fcb98e9af30d6ec4a0cc/Screenshot+2025-05-04+at+16_48_18.png?expires=1776771000&signature=ba65ca4209cccac5e2c953d683a8ad4236094c787ee0319c4b5acd4444a11ab7&req=1N1vxV77qDZk2hL085ZhoS2XiJNERFVV9wMTAStBr6Y3wg%2Bxw43SMB6JvKgh%0Azg%3D%3D%0A)

</Accordion>

💡 **Pro Tip:** After changing your password, keep it safe in a password manager, such as Bitwarden, so you don't forget it.
