_Welcome to our guide on syncing your Business WhatsApp account with HireData. Please follow the instructions below and learn how to manually sync your app when needed._

To do that, you should first connect your Business WhatsApp account with HireData via the **[embedded sign-up](/apps/messaging/whatsapp/connect-with-whatsapp-embedded-sign-up)** or the **[system user](/apps/messaging/whatsapp/connect-with-whatsapp-embedded-sign-up)**.

## Step 1: Navigate to the Settings page and open the Apps menu

Login to your HireData account and open the Settings page via the [Settings] button above your name. Then, go to the Apps menu.

![Apps page](/images/intercom/41582672-Screenshot-2B2025-03-17-2Bat-2B09_44_07.png)

## Step 2: Select WhatsApp

You can open WhatsApp to see your connection(s) by clicking on the name or the [View app] button.

![WhatsApp connections](/images/intercom/41582714-Screenshot-2B2025-03-17-2Bat-2B09_44_29.png)

## Step 3:** **Sync the data

Click on the [Sync] button (the two arrows in a circular motion) located in the drop-down menu of the company's connection. After you have clicked on it, you will see the syncing progress.

![](/images/intercom/41582740-Screenshot-2B2025-03-17-2Bat-2B09_44_45.png)
