_Welcome to our guide on setting up WhatsApp in HireData. Follow along to learn each step of connecting the two platforms via the embedded sign-up._

## Step 1: Choose a Connection Method

You can either do this from your [Inbox] and then [WhatsApp], or navigate to the [Settings page], then [Apps], and open [WhatsApp].

![Creating or selecting a WhatsApp Business account](/images/intercom/23699831-Screenshot-2B2024-10-23-2B160600.jpg)

You will be presented with two options to connect your WhatsApp account to HireData:

- **Embedded Sign-Up**: This option allows you to use the WhatsApp Embedded Sign-Up feature for easier setup.
- **[System User](/apps/messaging/whatsapp/connect-with-whatsapp-embedded-sign-up)**: To connect your WhatsApp account via this option, you will need the **WhatsApp Business Account Id** and the **Access Token** (API key).

![Embedded Sign-Up vs System User](/images/intercom/23699845-Screenshot-2B2024-10-23-2B160645.jpg)

## Step 2: Grant HireData Access to WhatsApp Business

### 2.1. Once you select a connection method, you will be taken to Facebook, where you will need to log in to your Facebook account

![Facebook login page](/images/intercom/23700795-1-2Bstep.jpg)

### 2.2. Then you will be redirected to the permissions page, where you will be asked to connect your WhatsApp Business account to HireData

By continuing, you will need to agree to the [WhatsApp Business Terms of Service](https://www.whatsapp.com/legal/business-terms?fbclid=IwZXh0bgNhZW0CMTAAAR0O-c4JvRz5u9AKYZq_4QBZjltjfxac5AoPTh3E31K3Ho2TtD8l-JP4DiA_aem_-GN92crMv9g93Rp_HF4Q-w) and the [Meta Terms of Service](https://business.facebook.com/legal/terms).

![WhatsApp Business account permissions page](/images/intercom/23700821-Screenshot-2B2024-10-23-2B154735.jpg)

## Step 3: Fill in Your Business Information

You will need to complete the business information fields, including:

- **Business Portfolio**: Choose an existing portfolio or create a new one.
- **Business Name**: Type the name of your business.
- **Business Website or Profile Page**: If available, enter the URL of your company's website or social media profile.
- **Country**: Select the country where your business is based.
- **Address (optional): **You can also add your business address if you want.

Once done, click [Next] to proceed.

![Business information page](/images/intercom/23701899-new.jpg)

## Step 4: Create or Select your WhatsApp Business Account

If you already have a WhatsApp Business account, you will be able to select it at this stage, but if you don't, you will be asked to create a new one.

![Creating or selecting a WhatsApp Business account](/images/intercom/23702357-Screenshot-2B2024-10-23-2B154956.jpg)

### 4.1. Creating a New WhatsApp Business Account

Fill in the required fields, including:

- **WhatsApp Business Account Name**: Enter the name of your WhatsApp Business Account. It shouldn't be longer than 255 characters.
- **WhatsApp Business Display Name**: Ensure the display name matches your business name and adheres to [WhatsApp's display name guidelines](https://www.facebook.com/business/help/757569725593362).
- **Category**: Select the business category that best describes your business.

Click [Next] to continue.

![Creating a new WhatsApp Business account](/images/intercom/23702845-Screenshot-2B2024-10-23-2B155036.jpg)

## Step 5: Add a Phone Number for WhatsApp

### 5.1. Enter the phone number that will be linked to your WhatsApp Business account

- **Phone Number**: Select the correct country code and input your phone number.

If the phone number you are attempting to use is already linked to WhatsApp, you will need to either [purchase a new phone number](/apps/messaging/whatsapp/buy-a-phone-number-from-numero-for-your-whatsapp-account) or delete your WhatsApp account and try again. Remember that if you do the latter, your chat history will be deleted.

- **Verification**: Choose how you would like to verify the phone number via a text message or a phone call.

![Adding phone number for WhatsApp](/images/intercom/23703206-Screenshot-2B2024-10-23-2B155239.jpg)

### 5.2. Verify your phone number by entering the 6-digit code you will receive

![Verification page](/images/intercom/23798726-Untitled-design-2810-29.png)

## Step 6: WhatsApp Connected

### 6.1. Review HireData's access request

![Access request page](/images/intercom/23795609-Screenshot-2B2024-10-24-2B131229.jpg)

### 6.2. Confirmation message

If you have set up everything correctly, you will see this message at the end, and you will be ready to use HireData with WhatsApp!

![Confirmation page](/images/intercom/23795636-Screenshot-2B2024-10-24-2B133352.jpg)

### 6.3. Review the connection between WhatsApp and HireData

Simply navigate to [Settings] &gt; [Apps] &gt; [WhatsApp] and review if the connection between HireData and WhatsApp is successful.

![WhatsApp connection in HireData](/images/intercom/23798221-Untitled-2Bdesign-2B-288-29.png)

Now that you know all of the steps, visit our [WhatsApp sign-up page](https://app.hiredata.com/inbox/whatsapp) to get started on setting up your WhatsApp connection. If you experience any issues, you can also try connecting your account to HireData using a [system user sign-in](/apps/messaging/whatsapp/connect-with-whatsapp-embedded-sign-up).
