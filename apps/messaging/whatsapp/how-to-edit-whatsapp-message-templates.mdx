_WhatsApp message templates in HireData allow you to manage and update messages used in automations. This article walks you through how to edit existing message templates directly in HireData._

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the template you want to edit">
    Go to your **Settings** and open the **Messages** page. There you will see a list of your WhatsApp templates created in HireData or synced with your WhatsApp Business Manager.

    Select the template you want to edit from the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit your message template">
    Update the message content as needed. Use the preview screen on the right to see how your message will appear to recipients before saving.

    ![The message template editor with a live preview on the right](/images/intercom/146959675-Screenshot-2026-05-29-at-10_28_58.png)

    Once you're happy with your edits, click **Save** in the upper right corner to apply them.

    ![The Save button in the template editor](/images/intercom/146960062-Screenshot-2026-05-29-at-10_30_25.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
  Each template can only be edited once every **24 hours**. Plan your changes carefully before saving, as you won't be able to make further edits until the next day.

  If you try to adjust it multiple times within that timeframe, you will see an alert message on the template.
</Warning>

To learn more about how templates work and how they are approved, see [How WhatsApp Templates Work](/apps/messaging/whatsapp/how-whatsapp-templates-work).
