_Welcome to our manual on syncing your Carerix account with HireData. Please follow the instructions below, in order to manually sync objects, statuses, and users. This way, you can use a candidate status, custom field or match stage you've just created or updated within Carerix, without waiting for the daily sync between your accounts._

To do that, you should first [connect your Carerix](https://help.hiredata.com/en/articles/157034-connect-with-carerix) account with HireData.

We will automatically schedule a sync to import the users' data from Carerix to HireData once the connection is established. That is required so the users can automatically send different types of communication through our platform. Then we schedule daily resyncs in case you make any changes in your Carerix profile.

However, you can also manually sync when, for instance, you want to see the changes you have made in your Carerix environment reflected in your HireData profile right away. Here is how to do it:

<Accordion title="Step 1: Navigate to the Settings page">

Login to your HireData account and open the Settings page via the [Settings] button above your name.

![Settings button](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/14261744/0f56dc64088388323608d8e9/image.png?expires=1776771000&signature=8c20c83b3fe7e1aa481162bcf995c51dabdc40e1522d1050c0c13e752244b7a9&req=0dFtw1n9qDNk2hL085ZhoVFMTQ7D%2FaFzAUo%2F0%2FHZQEiLQ2tt8q50l%2Bj1UTSh%0Azw%3D%3D%0A)

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Step 2: Open the Apps menu">

![Apps menu](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/14261773/1d24b70a00fe4eb449c38602/image.png?expires=1776771000&signature=b2bbc6f4c1652cb6a3b12da9ffef3e5286cebc2ca0a9d52f4e4864f519c5a271&req=0dFtw1n9qzRk2hL085ZhoX%2BFouoyBBJDFQJl%2BKGGz76wMybkM6qdu4ItjSr1%0AEw%3D%3D%0A)

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Step 3: Select the Carerix app">

You can open the Carerix app either by clicking on the name or the [View app] button.

![View app button](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/14261782/83deee1f9e4b25f57c2b9766/image.png?expires=1776771000&signature=27b10b0ed974a334ea3b8477894235cde0f9a36e436470b6c5b4160ffa2a96ac&req=0dFtw1n9pDVk2hL085ZhofhpPojZhlFXGFiodQUGPbepDt5vHj%2FFGswCUinM%0ApA%3D%3D%0A)

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Step 4:** **Sync the data">

Click on the [Sync] button (the two arrows in a circular motion) located at the end of the company's connection. After you have clicked on it, you will see the progress of the syncing.

![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/14261799/286030ee7a1c8cb27d767534/image.png?expires=1776771000&signature=7cb69b804a108645390ac63a1bf1fd43be30ae43d3957ea83ba31a2e547286ba&req=0dFtw1n9pT5k2hL085ZhoarqjPAbJwdXJq%2FSVHATlxD%2BVxLletM2Ok9WAQEa%0AOg%3D%3D%0A)

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Pro Tip">

When you hit the [Timeline] button (the first of the four buttons), you can see what has been synced.

![Data sync timeline](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/14262026/077804aa50d2d789356ee633/image+%282%29.png?expires=1776771000&signature=017c3bba827c4cbd0e3e93d798d86a5db88f21d818c2bbe89db44c0545e6b0f5&req=0dFtw1r6rjFk2hL085ZhoT2%2FfvP%2Fi%2FRdFY5%2B1haPDXt9bdCVQCubgBoMXZMk%0ANw%3D%3D%0A)
