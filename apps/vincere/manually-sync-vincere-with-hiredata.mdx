_Welcome to our manual on syncing your Vincere account with HireData. Please follow the instructions below to manually sync objects, statuses, and users. This way, you can use a candidate status, custom field or match stage you've just created or updated within Vincere, without waiting for the daily sync between your accounts._

To do that, you should first [connect your Vincere](/apps/vincere/connect-with-vincere) account with HireData.

We will automatically schedule a sync to import the users' data from Vincere to HireData once the connection is established. That is required so the users can automatically send different types of communication through our platform. Then we schedule daily resyncs in case you make any changes in your Vincere profile.

However, you can also manually sync when, for instance, you want to see the changes you have made in your Vincere environment reflected in your HireData profile right away. Here is how to do it:

## Step 1: Navigate to the Settings page

Log in to your HireData account and open the **Settings** page via the [Settings] button above your name.

![Settings button](/images/intercom/49929731-image.png)

## Step 2: Open the Apps page

![Apps page in HireData](/images/intercom/49931441-Screenshot-2025-05-16-at-11_17_59-copy.png)

## Step 3: Select the Vincere app

You can open the Vincere app either by clicking on the name or the [View app] button.

![View App button](/images/intercom/49931582-Screenshot-2025-05-16-at-11_17_59.png)

## Step 4: Sync the data

Click on the [Sync] button located in the drop-down menu of the company's connection.

![Sync button](/images/intercom/49931695-Screenshot-2025-05-16-at-11_19_28.png)

After you have clicked on it, you will see the progress of the syncing.

![Data Sync Timeline](/images/intercom/49931731-Screenshot-2025-05-16-at-11_19_52.png)

Finally, when the sync has finished successfully, you should receive this message at the bottom of the Data Sync Timeline:

![Data Sync Timeline](/images/intercom/49931760-Screenshot-2025-05-16-at-11_20_16.png)
