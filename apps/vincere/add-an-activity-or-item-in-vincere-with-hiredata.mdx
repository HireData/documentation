_Welcome to our detailed guide on automatically adding an activity or item in Vincere with HireData. This tutorial is designed to be user-friendly and efficient, helping you to leverage the full potential of Vincere automations by HireData._

Before we start setting up this automated task, make sure you've [set up the connection with Vincere](https://help.hiredata.com/en/articles/126717-connect-with-vincere) and are familiar with [how to create a Vincere automation](https://help.hiredata.com/en/articles/148950-create-a-vincere-automation-in-hiredata) in HireData. Now let's start configuring this automated task.

## Step 1: Adding a task to your automation

### 1.1. In your automation, click on the [+] to open up the Building Block Panel

### 1.2. Click on [Add] in the Building Block Panel

![Building Block Panel](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/lk3ftrxl/70819499/53984b57e5d940693e9bed4f6cf8/image.png?expires=1776771000&signature=ec918604b92096a53b8362f6952ddb4bbffb6eada1dc20ec8de8701c5b260122&req=19VnxFH%2BpT5k2hL085ZhoYNCtqGwneyioGzQ19donPZQBr0paNjNBRZo6fih%0Avw%3D%3D%0A)

### 1.3. Choose Activity or Item Task

![Activity or Item Task](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/lk3ftrxl/70819730/d23f3039362c6701991158ec00b7/image.png?expires=1776771000&signature=ef4db6f1b70caa7f29899007b1f0a95772a08b923db33bc8fb525bd869dd1103&req=19VnxFH9rzdk2hL085ZhoQr0CdJiiFxUkiQYerbT4j0kUUBsag1iwsCzgHTP%0ABg%3D%3D%0A)

## Step 2: Choosing the Activity or Item type

These are the currently supported Types that you can choose from:

- Candidate
- Candidate URL Document
- Candidate Note
- Company
- Contact
- Job
- Meeting

_Select the activity or item type you'd like to add or log in Vincere:_

![Vincere Activity or Item types](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/lk3ftrxl/70821327/04b9f27fd6da2beefb908b2387f6/image.png?expires=1776771000&signature=77a5a9bd1c46328af4f5ddead6d8900e5fe6d65b7bb465eae453f2098ab5684c&req=19Vnx1n5rjBk2hL085ZhoRNuc98G1JF7SlMlMxseTej4WtHhpPAZhvxrmIk3%0AQw%3D%3D%0A)

## Step 3: Filling in the item's fields

Now, you can complete the activity's or item's fields. You have the option to incorporate variables by clicking the icon located in the top-right corner. Both static and dynamic data can be used to populate the task. Remember to click [Save Task] when finished.

_Click on the curly brackets for the Variables menu:_
