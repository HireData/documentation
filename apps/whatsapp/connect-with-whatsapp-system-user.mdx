---
title: "Connect with WhatsApp: System User"
description: "Connect your Business WhatsApp account to HireData for seamless communication"
---

_Welcome to our guide on setting up WhatsApp in HireData. Follow along to learn how to create a System user in your Meta Business Suite account and connect your WhatsApp to HireData via the System user sign-in._

In the event you’re logged in to multiple meta business accounts, the URLs in this tutorial may redirect to the wrong business account, as meta uses identifiers in the URLs to resolve which business account’s settings to show.

<Accordion title="1. Create a system user">

### 1.1. [Log in](https://business.facebook.com/business/loginpage) to your Meta Business Suite account

### 1.2. Head to [system users](https://business.facebook.com/latest/settings/system_users) in the settings

### 1.3. Create a new system user

Click the add button on the top right and give your system user the "Admin" role.

![Create system user page](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/lk3ftrxl/24236593/b3a6641925d9d9f07acdc812aebf/image%2B-288-29.png?expires=1776771000&signature=8f105a2104faf2eb72131df3ee180981c8ba03172b21386b5a1842d2ab7b518f&req=0tFtxl7%2FpTRk2hL085ZhoXBfa7Q%2Bzti8fwYLdih5SZ74q5%2BRFrsRsO5YXJba%0A%2Fw%3D%3D%0A)

1. _If an admin user already exists, use that account_![Disclaimer message](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/lk3ftrxl/24236609/be96d20707ab68bbf0272ca39f24/image%2B-287-29.png?expires=1776771000&signature=36577dcc0a4202329ecadb83131010305b4b6da8b921a1405f50f972ccbddbdf&req=0tFtxl78rD5k2hL085ZhoQTYBoAFn77dPo7he5Nq4LtxG7dm%2BsiTHnocxOt4%0A4Q%3D%3D%0A)
2. _It is possible to use an employee role also but it will limit the business management abilities_

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="2. Assigning WhatsApp to the system user">

Most assets can be assigned/managed directly when editing a system user, however, WhatsApp accounts are an exception to this.

### 2.1. Go to your [WhatsApp accounts](https://business.facebook.com/latest/settings/whatsapp_account)

### 2.2. Assign your WhatsApp account to your system user

1. _Click on the WhatsApp account you want to assign to your system user_![WhatsApp accounts selection](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/lk3ftrxl/24236806/6c33517a8426ab19ad22f5874f18/image+%289%29.png?expires=1776771000&signature=8da65a52d0ff65fa4ed82e4fc28039791bfd1266b4b5c0f496e1d716f6fad4ad&req=0tFtxl7yrDFk2hL085ZhoQyyPXn2Kr13WC6Nv6J7Nqz44yt9ZkWV78gxDXAb%0AVA%3D%3D%0A)
2. _Click the assign people button to open the assign dialogue_![Assign people button](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/lk3ftrxl/24236843/2c419a78ce5ad21b3695fdfe0c7c/image+%2810%29.png?expires=1776771000&signature=c3f39d3794bbfa195762d4165c0b7debbf1b2b81e72a91437fae5e17c328ce38&req=0tFtxl7yqDRk2hL085ZhoRlUM1qnGPFSEVe3d8qf2DmVEfrz82GKfkPDakQC%0AIQ%3D%3D%0A)
3. _Search for and select your system user_Scroll down and give the user full control, which allows HireData to manage everything related to this WhatsApp business account once connected.
4. _Click the assign button to assign the user_![Add people and assign permissions page](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/lk3ftrxl/24236877/48c3f44b812c3eee366a9f6cd545/image+%2811%29.png?expires=1776771000&signature=6cd96bab881ab7da16cfb41826b8942418d60adf46e8ae1089e557e6f718e702&req=0tFtxl7yqzBk2hL085ZhoVCdlm5IiUR5W8vJ9Xz72aq0X16cJTXn8FbiIk%2Fq%0Apw%3D%3D%0A)
5. _Go to the people tab and verify the system user is assigned to the WhatsApp account_![People tab](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/lk3ftrxl/24236901/d007be85ca96d2d69fd86ab819c5/image+%2812%29.png?expires=1776771000&signature=88d0e12e1d34054ab3b0b3c133a426e2a75d894c550772f5b855f8df9b0cae05&req=0tFtxl7zrDZk2hL085ZhoQZMFbHVLHvWPOjZ6va4Ebh34qLgoR5d9mNnh7SI%0AbQ%3D%3D%0A)

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="3. Create an app">

⚠️ If you’ve not made an app or used Facebook for developers before, you may be prompted to sign up for it before being able to create an app.

### 3.1. Follow the [app creation](https://developers.facebook.com/apps/create) flow

### 3.2. Add the WhatsApp product to the app

Further configuration is not needed, but to indicate you will use WhatsApp with this app, selecting the WhatsApp product is necessary.

### 3.3. Go back to the meta business suite and head to [apps](https://business.facebook.com/latest/settings/apps)

### 3.4. Assign the system user to the app

1. _Click on the app_
2. _Click on assign people_![Assign people button](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/lk3ftrxl/24342975/7faafecbf3842a09423b61784231/image%2B-2813-29.png?expires=1776771000&signature=ff304aff1cfbf2e186e2a0b83aab1999af1394a9d3426cead0ae9fc80e249697&req=0tFswVrzqzJk2hL085ZhoQzXTCH73hw34MwuQuhZt7Si7onUabErZo0Mofko%0Aww%3D%3D%0A)
3. _Select the system user_
4. _Give the system user full control_![Permissions page](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/lk3ftrxl/24343051/2fe523103ed5252a25bca6a6e9ec/image%2B-2814-29.png?expires=1776771000&signature=62b45af8c17bc2cbd45ae1bcf948977c1e8ddaf5c03268abc13ce8055f3c4b9e&req=0tFswVv6qTZk2hL085ZhoUc367veOA%2F4SCvgUrBM4e%2FJyyJ54XEktqHd7%2Bds%0AZQ%3D%3D%0A)
5. _Click Assign_

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="4. Generate a system user token">

### 4.1. Return to the [system user page](https://business.facebook.com/latest/settings/system_users)

### 4.2. Generate the token

1. _Select the system user_
2. _Verify it’s connected to the WhatsApp account_
3. _Click the **[Generate token]** button_![System users page with generate token button](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/lk3ftrxl/24343257/0f0b82b2b0b126eb39dd85b4cdae/image+%2815%29.png?expires=1776771000&signature=395f9b8f3a85c70a952157dba0793b8f9d9e3df2ec811007bbd6e9bb1600744f&req=0tFswVv4qTBk2hL085ZhoccufvaAOG8D4cRZUdnC2jiJoQ8PJfgHRkl7uab6%0Avw%3D%3D%0A)
4. _Select the app_![Selecting the app](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/lk3ftrxl/24343296/475da49e6858b2f18eb80bae8385/image+%2816%29.png?expires=1776771000&signature=ca29d0aa151c019188697abf429381a440d11db6a6b3317df951bf849207250e&req=0tFswVv4pTFk2hL085ZhoQ7rppVmeCsJaW4ZG3o1IyDRj6GJYmArTPK3NV4A%0AJw%3D%3D%0A)
5. _Set the token to never expire_If the token is set to expire, you will have to reconnect your WhatsApp account with HireData every time it expires.![Setting expiration](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/lk3ftrxl/24343337/de58c3c34026b57b12ea4796bd31/image+%2817%29.png?expires=1776771000&signature=bbfc7f93952aa3b7d8d125f25f143c684f2389be1dbb8dabd4d96eefb56b4e03&req=0tFswVv5rzBk2hL085ZhoRW3tNxduekdJGOz%2FWPEIEpeeGBkXzHg7enURIyq%0AOg%3D%3D%0A)
6. _Select the WhatsApp business management and business messaging permissions_![Assigning permissions](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/lk3ftrxl/24343461/36638b0381bb1f8e5e646727008b/image+%2818%29.png?expires=1776771000&signature=a8fdb6a4ee1aa089e63fd67fab7d28bc2f5215de00de639aa1daf5e1c3b0543f&req=0tFswVv%2BqjZk2hL085ZhoWSPqOFsKa%2FCLmcQmI9eGwVW18l4o9zCiITu2MJu%0Aow%3D%3D%0A)
7. _Generate the token and copy the token once it’s generated_![Generate token button](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/lk3ftrxl/24343492/af868532cfe70991d45d7e00a900/image+%2819%29.png?expires=1776771000&signature=2872d1cae673e6f6847ce09382f8705e1b7b5ff5b7c565dda93bc4efa7786442&req=0tFswVv%2BpTVk2hL085ZhoXjolJUoAV4DNP0GnYf9A3v1u0Xbr4c67gNtgWWc%0AKA%3D%3D%0A)

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="5. Connect with HireData">

### 5.1. Go to the [WhatsApp app page](https://app.hiredata.com/apps/whatsapp) in HireData

### 5.2. Click the [+ New Connection] Button

You can also click the **[Connect to WhatsApp]** button; both open the same window for convenience.

![New connection button in HireData](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/lk3ftrxl/24343819/50a66d232e306bdffc76271fcbc1/image+%2820%29.png?expires=1776771000&signature=df40bfd108e5dfb8635da4eeb2374f3fe6067caac6a2e56a5527904eb66f2887&req=0tFswVvyrT5k2hL085ZhodS4L3TFtEGcAHkEBaXdnM2mua0KN9RtD9xsBk6t%0A5g%3D%3D%0A)

### 5.3. Click on System User

1. _Fill in your business id, this can be found in any url of the meta business suite of your business with the **`business_id=&lt;business_id&gt;`** parameter_
2. _Enter your access token_
3. _Click the **[Connect]** button_

![System user sign in](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/lk3ftrxl/24343844/c844270c2f2304d6513aa20e72e8/image+%2821%29.png?expires=1776771000&signature=aa91834cf6f3c7aad2a8916e15e57321e6f581d4720a1aeada0d2b01ad08c03b&req=0tFswVvyqDNk2hL085ZhoSxxKcyUOXwUG%2FrtymBlSGskPQtYUJK7NevYduSN%0AoQ%3D%3D%0A)

### 5.4. Wait for the sync to complete

Once the sync is complete, you can start using WhatsApp within HireData.

![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/lk3ftrxl/24343908/5467ede1cd995ac514d253cc766d/image.gif?expires=1776771000&signature=38cc103c32688abf2cd71c76eec7e8645b09db58bf9107a8e90f8e2cdc39c25c&req=0tFswVvzrD9k2hL085ZhoWNIQICS6Mf8xJSiDR7LW7R1wXQW84cRi0k2W9hq%0AeA%3D%3D%0A)

</Accordion>

Now that you know all of the steps, visit our [WhatsApp sign-up page](https://app.hiredata.com/inbox/whatsapp) to get started on setting up your WhatsApp connection or try it via the [embedded sign-up](https://help.hiredata.com/en/articles/232224-connect-with-whatsapp-embedded-sign-up).
