title: "Manually sync WhatsApp with HireData"
description: "Learn how to effortlessly sync HireData with WhatsApp"
---

_Welcome to our guide on syncing your Business WhatsApp account with HireData. Please follow the instructions below and learn how to manually sync your app when needed._

To do that, you should first connect your Business WhatsApp account with HireData via the **[embedded sign-up](https://help.hiredata.com/en/articles/232224-connect-with-whatsapp-embedded-sign-up)** or the **[system user](https://help.hiredata.com/en/articles/234333-connect-with-whatsapp-system-user)**.

<Accordion title="Step 1: Navigate to the Settings page and open the Apps menu">

Login to your HireData account and open the Settings page via the [Settings] button above your name. Then, go to the Apps menu.

![Apps page](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/lk3ftrxl/41582672/cb84eb312a54f0f9338a97c034bb/Screenshot%2B2025-03-17%2Bat%2B09_44_07.png?expires=1776771000&signature=feb8ec9920e4f76f9bb5f7dc74a30f0f63b37a93307ce33caf7f573196f6a8eb&req=1NRqzVr8qzVk2hL085Zhoa%2Bps29KQ1JRhHeQmp7eq3ZckmH3kOdj7cGZdLjV%0Afg%3D%3D%0A)

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Step 2: Select WhatsApp">

You can open WhatsApp to see your connection(s) by clicking on the name or the [View app] button.

![WhatsApp connections](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/lk3ftrxl/41582714/c6e2873ee09886fd481fcd6750f6/Screenshot%2B2025-03-17%2Bat%2B09_44_29.png?expires=1776771000&signature=8104622f640b8540c85ac425c43a56b75c8c9d9a01f7648fc11bc6d50eeb00e4&req=1NRqzVr9rTNk2hL085Zhodrdg4w6WbK7QWCLWTYpq4IaQb6ZKDgohYO1evnZ%0AAA%3D%3D%0A)

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Step 3:** **Sync the data">

Click on the [Sync] button (the two arrows in a circular motion) located in the drop-down menu of the company's connection. After you have clicked on it, you will see the syncing progress.

![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/lk3ftrxl/41582740/d33d4f0aa1958f6e629a4ccebfe0/Screenshot%2B2025-03-17%2Bat%2B09_44_45.png?expires=1776771000&signature=7299fc5af483e5d44fc6e4851d6977022c79403408c20bd4236e1522e7c1bfa3&req=1NRqzVr9qDdk2hL085ZhoRohVvIObgvxg8U%2FRSL%2FBv5Oy1XQs0RNciBzIX4S%0AZw%3D%3D%0A)

</Accordion>
