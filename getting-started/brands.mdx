_Welcome to our tutorial, which will teach you how to add a brand to HireData. You will learn everything you need to know about setting up the company's basic information, such as name, slogan, values, and location, as well as adding your branding, such as a logo, banner, and colour palette. If you'd rather watch the process unfold than read the instructions, there's a video guide at the end._

<Accordion title="Step 1: Open the Brands menu">

Open the Settings page in HireData and then navigate to the Brands menu.

![Brands menu](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/14301479/2c0a1a8cc79f412bc7bda0c7/image.png?expires=1776771000&signature=b03e7e2105e806a49217c8bb77a3966d09c22289d4ce69443b407296ffcbe7c8&req=0dFsxVn%2Bqz5k2hL085ZhobMPjwgitJGyrpdzNvMBkdWbI4fOfGvUYlhF%2FAkH%0ARw%3D%3D%0A)

If it's your first time doing this, you won't see any brands there because you haven't created them yet, so continue reading to learn how to do it.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Step 2: Add your brand">

To start, click on the [New Brand] button:

![New Brand button](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/14301531/79c408e35a38a778c59538bf/image.png?expires=1776771000&signature=de029430788d9d065fdcf04a30f9adc9a759a113685c6f271a4ea25987864805&req=0dFsxVn%2FrzZk2hL085ZhoXWxwvQGatcAg9MI0fZZfzMN0HjAXi5hZ00R95o3%0Azg%3D%3D%0A)

**_2.1. Add the name of your brand_**

![Brand name field](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/14301581/08e63bb814955b26ac76f6ab/image.png?expires=1776771000&signature=f58934e6b54fcf9bf3346ee24b7bc99bb4d868e36c28c0d2006bde7f9a8c4834&req=0dFsxVn%2FpDZk2hL085ZhoaJhZal0AspO86BhCQO8hrqn8aMD%2FPtjHLxyqe6Z%0ADw%3D%3D%0A)

**_2.2. Include its location_**

To do that, your company should have a Google Business profile because this is an integration with Google Maps, so if your company's data is not there, you won't be able to add the location.

If your business already has an account, you can continue typing the brand name in the search bar. You will see one or a few results pop up, depending on whether your company has multiple addresses. In that case, it's important to add the main one, which will be its headquarters, and after that, you can add more locations if you want.

![Location results](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/14301613/29b3c61838911eff3940e04d/image.png?expires=1776771000&signature=3ee9513ea2600c01b27c66a07cb6f586ad73da29caacde65b7859dc8aa137cae&req=0dFsxVn8rTRk2hL085Zhod5w%2FktjSlScX236knAxJ24YaElP4BpwqtMpbX%2BP%0A2A%3D%3D%0A)

**_2.3. Confirm creating your brand_**

Hit the [Create Brand] button at the bottom of the page in order to do that.

![Create brand button](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/14301658/b14dd5582da5479fcb20fbee/image.png?expires=1776771000&signature=9a5cb5d5059247568582cb7e1b7aaa2ba7f2c0d6642766ea406573a6d4f952d9&req=0dFsxVn8qT9k2hL085Zhob6u6%2FuMmXUfQl1O3td8j6FMls%2FSiqiP539coI4q%0AqQ%3D%3D%0A)

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Step 3: Set up the brand profile">

_In this section, we explain how to set up your brand profile, including the general information you need to add, importing a logo and a banner for the emails, reviewing the location, and lastly, including links to the brand's social media accounts that will be displayed in the sent communication._

<Accordion title="3.1. General settings">

**_3.1.1. Check the brand name and add a slogan_**

![Brand Name and Slogan fields](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/14302278/baa187b054d171f7eb33a285/image.png?expires=1776771000&signature=c1a42da410323c61083a4d714433071a7972c60b3451743a7f47a88e2d074f8f&req=0dFsxVr4qz9k2hL085ZhoT6MmXHbGNnDIeuvJ9yCXu01ZITXsJvh1ViCqfNV%0AGA%3D%3D%0A)

**_3.1.2. Add an avatar_**

This is typically the brand's logo, so click on the [Edit] icon and add it from your device.

**_3.1.3. Add a banner_**

Again, click on the [Edit] icon positioned on the banner's frame, and select the image from your device.

In some cases, the automatic shape might differ from the shape of your image, but you can easily fix that—just select [Crop shape] and then [Custom] to adjust it.

<Accordion title="3.2. Email settings">

In the email settings, you need to add a banner and logo which will be used in the campaigns you are going to create.

**_3.2.1. Add email banner_**

![Adding Email Banner ](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/14303285/1fbae095b526b6717adb2f72/image.png?expires=1776771000&signature=8bb6d80d28132dbc1f558c2860907b46e06788f57c72697b7f2ce21979ad20f3&req=0dFsxVv4pDJk2hL085ZhoRqdtbzxXlb3bQlc5DEHGDpEQjmlX5zU30TKBNAC%0A4A%3D%3D%0A)

**_3.2.2. Add email logo_**

The logo image should be a PNG file with a transparent background.

**Pro Tip:** Use a darker version of your logo because most email backgrounds are white and your logo won't be visible if it's in light colours. For example, if you have a white and a black version of your logo, always choose the black version for emails.

![Adding Email Logo](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/14303303/3c72817321b5812608a9bb6c/image.png?expires=1776771000&signature=3c7c491992c8ddd7347219e5d20567e413664654eaa7fa8ab04600511c5fd600&req=0dFsxVv5rDRk2hL085ZhoZDtH2FJjnSQzEEw8JvXethZhvJijtKPkJyGcykA%0ANA%3D%3D%0A)

**_3.2.3. Review and save_**

![Email settings overview](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/14303322/a0cf0d5cbaa151ff21b20285/image.png?expires=1776771000&signature=1bb3b8089b6a74b1d7a8e1b98c3a0c80929d6f3f7bde573290819db2881312f0&req=0dFsxVv5rjVk2hL085ZhoRXnCyZQ8nBDYkALqBHbj4A6mG6VZ1b4XB6rkgH4%0Afg%3D%3D%0A)

<Accordion title="Step 4: Create a colour palette">

Creating a colour palette for your brand in HireData allows you to incorporate your brand's colours into the design of your campaigns so that they are consistent with your business branding.

You can manually add them by typing the HEX colour codes or automatically extract the colours from one of the images you previously added, e.g. logo or banner.

![Creating a colour palette with HEX codes](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/14302790/363795a423d67ceaec99314a/image.png?expires=1776771000&signature=8dc75f9562c430a53895d908e54d970493a5a38d8fa56f82de50233098f77d9a&req=0dFsxVr9pTdk2hL085ZhocNsB6%2FTNP8%2BDmKiiwvcujlphvWyURJ23XM782k6%0A1A%3D%3D%0A)

If you decide to use this handy feature, double-check if the extracted colours are the same as your branding. In case, there is a difference, you can easily adjust them and then save the palette via the [Save palette] button.

</Accordion>
