_In this guide, we will walk you through everything you need to know about HireData templates. From creating and managing your own templates to utilising pre-built examples, we will cover all the essential aspects to help you make the most of this feature._

<Accordion title="Step 1: Open the Templates menu">

Navigate to the settings page and then click on [Templates], where you will see a list of different templates we have prepared for you.

![Templates tab](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/14942263/e55d7a2e7258f4f2755a27ed/image.png?expires=1776771000&signature=68bbe1c50c71b7a6bb45cebc595caf36238712a202f5f1eedf87ec354bd72b8b&req=0dFmwVr4qjRk2hL085ZhoZtbQJrr4fLQ1I0%2B7mYW9toFzt6wowvnVGTf4Maj%0A2w%3D%3D%0A)

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Step 2: Create a Template">

**_2.1. To start the process, click the [New Template] button at the top right corner and select the type of template you want to create_**

![New Template button](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/14942320/a7ac89673241c3d244fcb20f/image.png?expires=1776771000&signature=e91c3323946d9a598f0256230653d8b3179ead435ca6c41b217c4b769bfbad82&req=0dFmwVr5rjdk2hL085ZhoS9Rfa0qDQdHLO%2FU9uyoOnPf%2BwVg0OOXujAUNf1K%0A9Q%3D%3D%0A)

There are two types of templates that you can create: **Blank Email (HTML)** or **Blank Email (MJML)**.

![Mail templates](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/14942429/fb8fe4f14cd800aeded17179/image.png?expires=1776771000&signature=cbe210f71836e0f981559a020d6dbd499567a6c86c23c24f992991dfdcc76d0b&req=0dFmwVr%2Brj5k2hL085ZhoU7R10TBQrcGILsfJpCQnXa00TtDWGOYrgjF%2FaEn%0ANA%3D%3D%0A)

In this guide, we will focus on creating a Blank Email (HTML) template.

**_2.2. Name your template_**

![Template name box](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/14942464/675aad78edcc03cc0e59a242/image.png?expires=1776771000&signature=4596821213c9d2147c5c8b4d94b905c6e772828a1e8223e70c042ca300561ab6&req=0dFmwVr%2BqjNk2hL085ZhofoGAG9jHnr9YDGyIblgyU%2Br9edPHWXr0PnjdiyZ%0AsA%3D%3D%0A)

**_2.3. Build your template_**

On the right side, there is a code editor where you need to paste the HTML code you have written. After you have inserted the code, a preview of your template will be displayed on the left side of the screen.

![Code editor and preview screen](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/14942493/fde18c2198e7eb05592e10aa/image.png?expires=1776771000&signature=0e305ecc2e0287ba054358c5194ad4b12e117f5b12c606fcf443913532f16afd&req=0dFmwVr%2BpTRk2hL085ZhoZR9FackkH%2FtCAIIlb2L9Bibj6x3qpmV3vVi%2BSJI%0A%2Fg%3D%3D%0A)

**_2.4. Add subject to the email_**

In the [Subject] area, you can add variables. There are two ways to do that. The first one is to open the variables on the right via the **\{\}** button. Then type any keyword for the variable to find it quicker, and simply click on it to copy.

![Variables menu](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/14942552/7d2212db720631f2dae061ec/image.png?expires=1776771000&signature=aa923a5b9d74f66e9ce211d38e8a24b80182eb5415ca76e89a0de6d929bf1390&req=0dFmwVr%2FqTVk2hL085ZhoUvX%2Fb0wMsEl6%2FiVNoomcMzQEvTkz378RVk0uJ0a%0AzQ%3D%3D%0A)

The other way to do this is to type "**\{\{**" followed by the variable's name in the subject field.

![Finding variables in the subject box](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/14942599/c9660c99e2c7270b547e292b/image.png?expires=1776771000&signature=8f067656016571be80cc455a377f880691a765136fd62860a7a10b9f783a1c42&req=0dFmwVr%2FpT5k2hL085ZhoYwO2emHj%2F2GNmqYATXPAFgFrxrOhyYUOtzPR5hB%0Aiw%3D%3D%0A)

![Subject example](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/14942625/e44e52f8eee2484348d70c11/image.png?expires=1776771000&signature=0e1e846f7c055683f6dac878eed8038d2493f3b16b19c7158810ffdb94430d9c&req=0dFmwVr8rjJk2hL085ZhoeBwFFeXqw5oCvMGe4NRdk9ESvwp0wYkY4UMfr0H%0ADw%3D%3D%0A)

**_2.5. Save your template_**

![Save button](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/14942644/91c78b115c483792d59ed578/image.png?expires=1776771000&signature=b79dd316c6fa05ad205ff207338a0506c97d22e987cd3be46d536eefbcb54c86&req=0dFmwVr8qDNk2hL085ZhoWqmj3WZsuhk1JunlGEDqB85zBQ%2BVmNY99bXE8J1%0A%2Fg%3D%3D%0A)

**_3.2. Select the template you want to use for the email_**

![Brands options](https://downloads.intercomcdn.eu/i/o/14942685/78a44396c72884d0111113c9/image.png?expires=1776771000&signature=f74a3f4f1f4712f7c3c0a59a0ae6488c2dc6a2c96db4c8acaf04d80a8c1b34d6&req=0dFmwVr8pDJk2hL085ZhoRr5BDxzQACb9ZqGy4BkX2GH3%2FVNwVdhkRdLTfeC%0ABA%3D%3D%0A)
